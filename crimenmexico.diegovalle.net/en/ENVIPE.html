Underreporting crime

— ENVIPE
— SESNSP victims/cars 



The Encuesta Nacional de Victimización y Percepción sobre Seguridad Pública (ENVIPE) is national victimization survey that allows us to compare crimes reported to the justice system with all crimes committed in the country. Blue lines are crimes reported to the justice system and magenta the estimates of total crimes in Mexico via survey. The 'unrecorded' crime rate (the percentage or number of offences which is not notified the police) was 93.6% at the national level in 2016 and 93.7% in 2015.
